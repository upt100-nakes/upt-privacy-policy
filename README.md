#Privacy Policy for UPT (Upaya Peduli Tuberkulosis)

**Last Updated:** March 27, 2026

UPT ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information within the UPT mobile application.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
UPT is designed to serve as a local tool to assist with medication management. We store the following data to provide core features:
* **Treatment Schedule:** Medication name, dosage, and time entered by the user.
* **Appointment Data:** Date and time for medical examinations.
* **User Preferences:** Technical settings such as alarm ringtone, vibration status, reminder interval, and theme (Light/Dark Mode).

### 2. Data Storage (Local Only)
**Your data never leaves your device.**
* **No Cloud Sync:** UPT does not use external servers. All data is stored locally on your device's internal storage using Room Database and DataStore.
* **No Data Sharing:** Because we do not collect your data on any servers, we do not share your information with any third parties.
* **Data Deletion:** Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all saved medication schedules and preferences.

### 3. Required Permissions
* **Notifications & Alarms:** To trigger reminders at the scheduled time.
* **Precise Alarm Permission:** To ensure medical reminders are accurate and not delayed by system battery saving features.
* **Battery Optimization & Autostart:** Required to ensure the alarm service remains active in the background for medication discipline.

### 4. Children's Privacy
Our app is not designed to collect personal information from children.

* **No Personal Data Collection:** UPT does not knowingly collect or solicit personally identifiable information (PII) from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

* **Local Use by Minors:** If a child uses this app to manage their medication, all data remains on the device. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's mobile app usage to ensure a safe experience.
* **Compliance:** If we learn that we have inadvertently collected data on our servers (which our app is not designed to do), we will immediately take steps to delete that information.

### 5. Security
We use standard Android security protocols to ensure your local data is protected from unauthorized access by other apps on your device.
